# Prohibited Activities List

*Last Updated: March 7, 2026*

The following activities are prohibited when using BIX Services or the BIX Card. This list is not exhaustive, and BIX reserves the right to update it at any time.

## Illegal or Unlawful Activities

- Money laundering, terrorist financing, or proliferation financing
- Transactions involving the proceeds of illegal activity
- Fraud, identity theft, or impersonation
- Operating an unlicensed money services business or money transmission business
- Tax evasion or facilitating tax evasion
- Human trafficking, forced labor, or exploitation
- Production, distribution, or sale of illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Child exploitation material or services
- Unlicensed gambling operations
- Activities that violate applicable sanctions, embargoes, or export controls

## Sanctions and Restricted Parties

- Transactions with or for the benefit of individuals or entities on the Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list maintained by the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)
- Transactions with or for the benefit of persons or entities in comprehensively sanctioned countries or regions
- Transactions that would cause BIX, its partners, or its service providers to violate any applicable sanctions laws or regulations

## Financial and Market Manipulation

- Market manipulation, wash trading, spoofing, or layering
- Front-running or insider trading
- Ponzi schemes, pyramid schemes, or multi-level marketing fraud
- Pump-and-dump schemes involving crypto assets or securities
- Price manipulation of any kind

## Prohibited Merchant Categories and Transactions

- Internet gambling, online casinos, or sports betting (except where explicitly permitted by applicable law and approved by BIX)
- Purchase of lottery tickets through unauthorized channels
- Adult content or services (except where legal and in compliance with applicable law)
- Weapons, ammunition, or explosives (except where legal and properly licensed)
- Counterfeit goods or services
- Unauthorized sale of prescription medications or medical devices

## Abuse of the Platform

- Creating multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions or limits
- Using the Services on behalf of a third party without authorization
- Reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling any part of the Services
- Attempting to gain unauthorized access to BIX systems, accounts, or data
- Using bots, scripts, or automated tools to interact with the Services without authorization
- Circumventing or attempting to circumvent any security, compliance, or risk controls
- Filing false or fraudulent chargebacks or disputes
- Structuring transactions to avoid reporting thresholds or detection

## Restricted Jurisdictions

- Using the Services from or on behalf of persons located in jurisdictions where BIX has determined it cannot lawfully provide Services
- Misrepresenting your location, identity, or jurisdiction of residence

---

If you become aware of any prohibited activity on the BIX platform, please report it to [support@bix.fi](mailto:support@bix.fi).

Violation of this Prohibited Activities List may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account, forfeiture of funds, and referral to law enforcement authorities where appropriate.
